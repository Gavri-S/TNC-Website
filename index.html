About Us

Selling a business is about more than numbers—it’s about people, legacy, and the future. Two Nine Capital understands that. TNC's dynamic team and flexible approach preserve what is special about each business while unlocking new opportunities for growth.

Our Kansas City roots shape our goals, and our executive-level experience, deep connections to local leaders, and world-class credentials ensure we can execute. Backed by continued mentorship from Kansas City’s most accomplished executives, we provide the stability, energy, and strategic vision necessary for a seamless transition.

Strategy

We acquire profitable, well-positioned businesses with strong cash flow and multi-million dollar revenue, headquartered within 250 miles of Kansas City. Our focus is on family-owned and owner-operated companies in industries where location drives competitive advantage. With a flexible approach, we work alongside leadership or step in as operators to drive sustainable growth while preserving company culture.
